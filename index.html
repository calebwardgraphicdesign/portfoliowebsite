About Me

My name is Caleb Ward. I have five years of experience with freelance and academic design work in web design, aerial footage capture, photography, and Adobe Creative Cloud software. I love all things technology and am always looking to learn more and tackle new projects. I am a quick learner and want to see everyone succeed.

Professional social media video edits, fast website creation/updates, complete business branding, and drone and photography services are all things I am confident in providing. Whether working with a local business or doing complete remote work, I strive to build good connections with everyone and make working with me as easy as possible. I look forward to working with new and old clients and hope that we can create something remarkable together.
